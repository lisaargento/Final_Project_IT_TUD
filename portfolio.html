Portfolio

You think "Another association which talk about environnement and solidarity, I'm not here to plant trees! ". Well we do, but not only. Throughout the year, we offer you a host of activities from the manufacture of homemade products to a debate about movie, including the organization of a solidarity thrift store or a disco-soup. It's up to you to see according to your time and your desires if you want to come to all of them, from time to time or even be at the initiative of new activities. We are here to encourage all initiatives that work for ecology and solidarity, so do not hesitate to come and see the team to share your ideas.

Thrift Store

We run this project to expand the life of clothes and help people buy less expansive clothes. This project was run with the Noisy-Liens association to raise money to support their missions.

Clean Walk

We believe that everyone who wants to help can and together it is easy to go outside and clean the city.

Disco-soup

Free soup is distributed to all student in need. Games and music.

Tree

Each year, during our internship in Forcalquier, we plant a tree there to help to keep the biodiversity in the site and to combat climate change.
